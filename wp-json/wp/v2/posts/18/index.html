There is a lot of curiosity in the technology world about the electric-powered pickup ‘Cyber Truck’ made by Tesla, the electric car manufacturer owned by Elon Musk. This time, the Vietnamese car manufacturer Vinfast has developed an electric-powered pickup named ‘VF Wild’. The company also showcased its electric-powered pickup at the Consumer Electronics Show in the United States.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Named VF Wild, the electric pickup has four doors, two rows of seating and a five-foot-long cargo bed. The passenger seat of the pickup can be folded inward when needed. As a result, it is possible to carry more than three feet of cargo in addition to the cargo bed.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

According to Vinfast, the VF Wild pickup was originally developed as an electric-powered pickup prototype. Future electric-powered pickups will follow this example. The pickup is not considered a rival to Tesla’s Cyber Truck due to its smaller size.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Apart from the VF Wild, Vinfast also showcased an electric SUV called the VF 3. The car is available in Vietnam and will soon go on sale in the US market. This two-door electric SUV is compact in size. The car can travel 125 miles on a single charge.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
